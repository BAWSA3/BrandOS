## About BrandOS

BrandOS is the operating system for brand consistency in the age of AI-generated content. As companies scale content production across channels, platforms, and creators, maintaining brand coherence becomes exponentially harder. BrandOS provides the infrastructure to define, protect, and evolve brand identity programmatically.

### Core Capabilities

- **Brand DNA System** - Define colors, tone, keywords, and behavioral patterns
- **Content Check Engine** - Real-time brand alignment scoring
- **AI Content Generation** - On-brand content at scale
- **Design Intent Blocks** - Natural language to design rules
- **Taste Translation** - Convert subjective feedback into actionable rules
- **Platform Adaptation** - Optimize for every channel automatically
- **Creator Guardrails** - Enable teams while protecting the brand
- **Brand Memory** - Learn and improve from every interaction
