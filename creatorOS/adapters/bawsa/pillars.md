## Pillar Detection Rules

When classifying an idea, check for:

1. **Primary keywords** from each pillar's keyword list
2. **Topic alignment** with the categories listed
3. **Audience fit** - who would care most about this?

**If unclear**, default to asking:
> "This could be a [Pillar A] post or a [Pillar B] post. Which angle do you want to take?"

**Cross-pillar signals:**
- Contains keywords from 2+ pillars
- Could appeal to multiple audiences
- Natural bridge between topics
