# Growth Plan Summary — 33K to 50K

## The Math
- Current: 33,000 followers
- Target: 50,000 followers
- Deadline: mid-July 2026 (~4 months)
- Needed: 17,000 new followers = ~4,250/month = ~142/day

## The Problem: Content Doesn't Travel

| Metric | Current | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Total retweets (20 posts) | 23 | 100-200+ |

| Retweet-to-like ratio | 1.9% | 8-15% |

**Distribution problem, not quality problem.**

## The 4 Levers

1. **Fix CTAs** (highest impact) — every post gets an explicit ask
2. **Threads** (high impact) — 2/week minimum, 5-7 tweets each
3. **Shareable formats** (medium-high) — frameworks, data posts, quote RT bait
4. **Strategic reach** (medium) — replies on large accounts, spaces, hashtags

## What to Stop

1. Bare links with no context (0.5-3.5% vs 5.8% avg engagement)
2. Posts without CTAs
3. Relying only on single tweets
