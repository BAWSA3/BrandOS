# Content Formats Guide

## Post 1 Formats (Anchor Posts)

### Thread (Tuesday + Friday)
- 5-7 tweets
- Tweet 1: bold claim or hook (this IS the content — must stand alone)
- Tweet 2-5: proof, examples, framework
- Tweet 6: summary + CTA
- Tweet 7: "follow @BawsaXBT for more on [topic]" + pin note
- Always end with RT tweet 1 + follow CTA
- Target: 2x the single tweet impression average (5,000+)

### Framework Post (Monday)
- Single tweet
- Present a clear, numbered or labeled system
- Examples: "the 3-2-1 rule", "the 5-layer brand stack", "the content audit framework"
- CTA: "save this" or "bookmark for later"
- Make it a gift to the sharer — they look smart reposting it

### Hot Take (Wednesday)
- Single tweet
- Start with "unpopular opinion:" or a bold claim
- Must be genuinely polarizing (not fake controversy)
- CTA: "agree or disagree?"
- These drive the most replies — lean in

### Behind the Scenes / Build Log (Thursday)
- Single tweet
- Reference specific features, late-night coding sessions, real metrics
- Include timestamps ("2:47am. still coding.")
- CTA: "reply with what you're building" or "reply with yours"
- Authenticity + execution = influence

### Community Engagement (Saturday)
- Single tweet
- Question format or "drop your handle" style
- Designed to generate high reply volume
- CTA: "drop it below" or "reply with yours"
- Use #buildinpublic

### Reflection (Sunday)
- Single tweet
- Bigger-picture thinking about building, branding, or the creator journey
- CTA: "quote RT with your take" or "quote RT with your phase"

## Post 2 Formats (Lighter Posts)

### Observation Tweet
- Repurpose an insight from that day's Post 1 or recent thread
- Shorter, more conversational
- CTA: reply trigger or agree/disagree

### Build Log
- Quick update on what you shipped/coded
- Include a specific detail (feature name, metric, time)
- CTA: "reply with yours"

### Quick Data Insight
- One stat or finding, presented simply
- "posts with X get Y% more Z"
- CTA: "save this"

### Quote RT Bait
- Share a take designed to invite people to add their perspective
- "the worst advice I've gotten about X: [take]. quote with yours"
- CTA: "quote this with yours"

### Value Offer (1x per week max)
- Offer something free in exchange for a reply
- "reply 'audit' and I'll review your last 10 posts"
- "reply 'system' and I'll send you my framework"
- CTA: reply trigger keyword

### Weekend Personal
- More human, less tactical
- Honest questions, genuine reflections
- CTA: "reply honestly"

### Week Recap (every Sunday Post 2)
- Summarize the week's posting results
- Reference specific wins or learnings
- CTA: "follow if you want to see month X"
