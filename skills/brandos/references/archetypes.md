# BrandOS Creator Archetypes

## The 8 Archetypes

Each creator maps to ONE primary archetype based on their content patterns,
voice, and positioning. Archetypes are not labels — they're strategic
identities that inform content strategy.

---

### 🎓 THE PROFESSOR
**Tagline:** "the one who makes you smarter"

**Signals:**
- Educational threads and breakdowns
- Framework reveals and mental models
- Data-backed insights and analysis
- "Here's how it actually works" energy
- Structured, clear writing style

**Typical Score Profile:** High DEFINE, High CHECK, Medium-High GENERATE
**Tier:** 4 (advanced)

**Example Creators:** Sahil Bloom, James Clear, Shaan Puri (edu mode)

---

### 🔌 THE PLUG
**Tagline:** "the one who connects everyone"

**Signals:**
- Curates and amplifies others' work
- Community-first approach
- "You should follow @..." or "underrated account" posts
- Networking energy, always tagging people
- Builds in public but centers the community

**Typical Score Profile:** High SCALE, Medium DEFINE, High GENERATE
**Tier:** 3 (established)

**Example Creators:** Justin Welsh (connector mode), community builders

---

### 🎪 CHIEF VIBES OFFICER
**Tagline:** "the one who makes the timeline fun"

**Signals:**
- Entertainment-first content
- Memes, shitposts, cultural commentary
- Personality-driven (you follow for THEM, not the topic)
- High engagement through humor and relatability
- Often breaks format — unpredictable in a good way

**Typical Score Profile:** High CHECK (consistent voice), High SCALE (viral), Variable DEFINE
**Tier:** 2 (emerging)

**Example Creators:** Dril, internet personality accounts

---

### 🔮 THE PROPHET
**Tagline:** "the one who sees what's coming"

**Signals:**
- Strong, opinionated takes on the future
- Philosophical and visionary content
- "Mark my words" energy
- Contrarian positions defended with logic
- Long-form thinking, manifestos, predictions

**Typical Score Profile:** Very High DEFINE, High CHECK, Medium GENERATE
**Tier:** 5 (peak — terminal archetype)

**Example Creators:** Naval Ravikant, Balaji, Paul Graham

---

### 🚢 SHIP OR DIE
**Tagline:** "the one who builds in public"

**Signals:**
- Constant shipping updates
- Build logs, progress screenshots, metrics sharing
- Product-focused (always talking about what they're making)
- "Shipped this today" / "Just launched" energy
- Transparent about wins AND failures

**Typical Score Profile:** High GENERATE, High DEFINE, Medium SCALE
**Tier:** 3 (established)

**Example Creators:** Pieter Levels, indie hackers, builders

---

### 🐕 UNDERDOG ARC
**Tagline:** "the one on the come-up"

**Signals:**
- Raw energy, growing fast
- Documenting the journey (not the destination)
- "Started from zero" narrative
- High output but still finding their voice
- Relatable — audience roots for them

**Typical Score Profile:** High GENERATE (posts a lot), Medium-Low DEFINE (still finding it), Medium CHECK
**Tier:** 1 (entry — every creator starts here)

**Example Creators:** Early-stage creators, anyone in their first year

---

### 🎰 THE DEGEN
**Tagline:** "the one who bets big"

**Signals:**
- High-risk takes and positions
- Crypto/trading/speculation energy
- YOLO vibes, unapologetic
- Mixes alpha with entertainment
- Fast-paced, reactive content

**Typical Score Profile:** High SCALE (engagement), Variable DEFINE, Medium CHECK
**Tier:** 2 (emerging)

**Example Creators:** Crypto twitter personalities, traders

---

### 👻 THE ANON
**Tagline:** "the one you can't identify but can't ignore"

**Signals:**
- Pseudonymous identity (anime PFP, abstract avatar)
- Authority without personal brand
- Content speaks for itself — no personal story needed
- Often technical or niche expertise
- Mysterious but consistent

**Typical Score Profile:** High CHECK (strong voice), High DEFINE (clear niche), Low SCALE (doesn't optimize for growth)
**Tier:** 2 (emerging)

**Example Creators:** Anon crypto accounts, pseudonymous tech accounts

---

## Archetype Selection Rules

1. **One archetype only.** Pick the strongest signal. Don't hedge.
2. **Crypto context matters.** If the creator is clearly in crypto/web3, weight
   The Degen and The Anon higher in consideration.
3. **Follower count doesn't determine archetype.** A 500-follower account can
   be The Professor. A 100K account can be Underdog Arc (if they grew fast
   and haven't defined their brand yet).
4. **When in doubt, go with voice.** The archetype should match HOW they
   communicate, not just WHAT they talk about.
5. **Underdog Arc is the default for unclear signals.** If you genuinely can't
   determine a clear archetype, default to Underdog Arc — it implies growth
   potential, not weakness.

## Evolution Paths (for reference — this is tracked in the full product)

```
Underdog Arc → [any archetype]
The Degen → Ship or Die, The Prophet
The Anon → The Prophet, The Professor
Chief Vibes Officer → The Plug, The Prophet
The Plug → The Prophet, The Professor
Ship or Die → The Professor, The Prophet
The Professor → (terminal)
The Prophet → (terminal / peak)
```

Archetypes only evolve UPWARD. The full BrandOS product tracks this over time.
The skill shows the current snapshot only.
