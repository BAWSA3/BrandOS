# BrandOS Scoring Rubric

## The 4 Dimensions

### DEFINE (30% weight) — Brand Clarity
How clearly does this person communicate WHO they are and WHAT they're known for?

**Score 80-100:**
- Bio instantly communicates value prop
- Consistent theme across all posts (1-3 clear pillars)
- Clear positioning — you know what they stand for
- Would pass the "cover the name" test (you'd recognize the author)

**Score 60-79:**
- Bio is decent but could be sharper
- Themes are present but sometimes wander
- Positioning exists but isn't razor-sharp

**Score 40-59:**
- Bio is generic ("entrepreneur | investor | dreamer")
- Themes are scattered — posts about too many things
- Hard to explain what they're "about" in one sentence

**Score 0-39:**
- Bio is empty or irrelevant
- No consistent themes — random mix of topics
- Zero clear positioning

### CHECK (25% weight) — Voice Consistency
Does this person sound like THEMSELVES consistently?

**Score 80-100:**
- Recognizable writing style (could identify without seeing the name)
- Consistent tone, cadence, formatting across posts
- Signature patterns (phrases, structures, punctuation habits)
- Voice matches the archetype they're projecting

**Score 60-79:**
- Generally consistent voice with occasional drift
- Some recognizable patterns emerging
- Tone is mostly stable

**Score 40-59:**
- Voice shifts noticeably between posts
- Mix of AI-sounding and authentic content
- No signature patterns yet

**Score 0-39:**
- No consistent voice at all
- Posts could be from different people
- Heavy AI/template usage obvious

### GENERATE (25% weight) — Content System
Do they have a system, or are they posting randomly?

**Score 80-100:**
- Regular posting cadence (4+ times/week minimum)
- Format variety (threads, singles, data posts, stories)
- Strong hooks — first lines stop the scroll
- Content pillars are clear and rotated intentionally

**Score 60-79:**
- Somewhat regular posting (2-3 times/week)
- Uses 2+ formats but could diversify
- Hooks are decent, not elite
- Content pillars exist but rotation is loose

**Score 40-59:**
- Inconsistent posting (gaps of days/weeks)
- Mostly one format (all single tweets, or all threads)
- Weak hooks — doesn't grab attention
- No clear content system

**Score 0-39:**
- Posts sporadically
- One format only
- No hooks — just statements
- No system whatsoever

### SCALE (20% weight) — Growth Signals
Is this brand set up to grow, or just maintaining?

**Score 80-100:**
- Strong CTAs in most posts (reply, RT, save, tag)
- High engagement relative to follower count (3%+ rate)
- Growing follower trend
- Distribution signals (getting retweets, quote tweets)
- Cross-platform presence or strategic collaborations

**Score 60-79:**
- CTAs in some posts but not consistent
- Decent engagement (1-3% rate)
- Stable follower count
- Some distribution but could be stronger

**Score 40-59:**
- Rare CTAs
- Low engagement relative to followers (<1%)
- Flat or declining followers
- Minimal distribution — likes but no shares

**Score 0-39:**
- No CTAs ever
- Very low engagement
- Declining or stagnant
- Zero distribution strategy

## Tier Adjustments

Apply these adjustments AFTER computing the raw weighted score:

### Follower Tier Bonus
- 0-1K followers: +0 (baseline)
- 1K-10K: +2 (growing, some signal)
- 10K-50K: +3 (established presence)
- 50K-100K: +4 (significant reach)
- 100K+: +5 (major account)

### Authority Signals (add up to +5)
- Verified / notable: +2
- Referenced by others frequently: +1
- Has a product/company: +1
- Cross-platform presence: +1

### Penalty Signals (subtract up to -5)
- Engagement farming (low quality engagement): -2
- Inconsistent posting (>2 week gaps): -2
- Generic AI content obvious: -3
- Bought followers (high count, zero engagement): -5

## Final Score = min(100, max(0, raw_weighted + tier_bonus + authority - penalties))
